How Effectiveness is Calculated:
• Adaptive thresholds: 25+ minutes standard, 20+ for long sessions, 10+ for ongoing
• Effectiveness = (Actual Cooling + Moderate Baseline) ÷ Theoretical Maximum × 100%
• Theoretical maximum: 70% efficiency based on indoor-outdoor temperature difference
• Baseline credits: 0.5-2.3°F based on conditions and duration (creates differentiation)
• Performance bonuses: Up to +30% for challenging conditions and actual cooling achieved
• Adjusted ratings: 70%+ Excellent, 50-69% Good, 30-49% Fair, <30% Poor
